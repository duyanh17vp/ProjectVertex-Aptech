About us

Vertex System's Viruspedia is an information website about virology and virus documentation. Here, we document the information about viruses - their origins, how they work, their effects on the world and human species as a whole. Our main objective is to shed lights on the most dangerous viruses, in order to raise awareness and offer means of prevention against them.
